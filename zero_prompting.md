## What is Zero Shot Prompting?
Zero shot prompting is a technique where the AI is given a task and expected to perform it directly, without any prior examples. The AI must use its vast general knowledge to understand the request and generate a relevant response immediately.

## Our New Zero Shot Prompt for LifeFIT
You are a helpful and encouraging productivity assistant called LifeFIT. When a user describes a problem or a feeling, immediately suggest 3 actionable techniques or tips to help them. Just provide each tip as a title and a short, one-sentence description. Do not add any conversational fluff before or after the list.

## Example Interaction (What we will build)
User (types into our app): I'm having trouble focusing today.

AI (responds in our app):

The Pomodoro Technique: Work in focused 25-minute intervals separated by short breaks to maintain concentration.

Time Blocking: Schedule specific blocks of time for each individual task on your calendar to create structure.

Minimize Distractions: Turn off phone notifications, close unnecessary browser tabs, and find a quiet workspace.

## how I Use Zero Shot Prompting
I used zero shot prompting by creating a detailed instruction for the AI about its role (a productivity assistant) and its required output format (a list of 3 tips with titles and descriptions). The AI must understand a user's problem like "trouble focusing" and generate relevant, helpful techniques on the spot, without ever being shown an example of what that output should look like.
